Who We Are

VoltEdge Supply is a lean, independent reseller specializing in ABB low‑voltage components for commercial applications. We help MRO teams, facility managers, and contractors source the right parts quickly—without markup games or runaround.

Built for MRO Speed

  • Fast, clear quotes (typically in < 24 hours)
  • Quote‑first, dropship‑friendly fulfillment
  • No minimum order quantity (No MOQ)
  • Documentation on request (datasheets, cross‑references)

How We’re Different

  • ABB‑focused catalog: S200 breakers, AF contactors, MS motor starters, OT disconnects, OVR surge protection.
  • Trust over hype: Plain‑English quotes, itemized pricing, and realistic lead times.
  • Right‑sized operation: We stay lean so your pricing stays sharp.

How We Work

  1. Send a request with part numbers, quantities, and need‑by date.
  2. We source & confirm availability/lead time with our suppliers.
  3. Approve & pay a clean, itemized invoice.
  4. We ship directly to your dock or job site.

Note: VoltEdge Supply operates as an independent reseller. Brand names (e.g., ABB) are used for identification only.
